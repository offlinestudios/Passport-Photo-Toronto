  • Recent photograph taken within the last 6 months
  • Color photo with a white background

  • No head coverings (except for religious reasons)

What is an OCI Card?

The Overseas Citizenship of India (OCI) card is a document that provides immigration benefits to people of Indian origin. It offers:

  • Multiple entry, multi-purpose, lifelong visa for visiting India
  • Exemption from registering with local authorities for any length of stay
  • Parity with Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) in economic, financial, and educational fields
  • Parity with NRIs regarding inter-country adoption of Indian children
  • Parity with resident Indians in matters of domestic air fares and entry fees to monuments

However, OCI cardholders do not have the right to vote, hold public office, or purchase agricultural properties in India.

OCI Application Information

While we specialize in OCI photos, here's some general information about OCI applications from Canada:

  • OCI applications from Canada are processed through BLS International Service Canada Inc.
  • Applications can be submitted in person or by mail to BLS centers in Toronto, Vancouver, Ottawa, and other Canadian cities
  • Processing times vary, but typically take 8-12 weeks for standard applications
  • Two identical photos are typically required for the application
  • The application process is now completely online, but physical photos are still required to be submitted with printed application forms

For the most current information about OCI applications, please visit the official BLS International website.
