Digital Passport Photo Requirements

Digital passport photos have specific requirements that differ from printed photos. Our service ensures your digital photos will comply with all these requirements:

  • JPEG/JPG format with appropriate dimensions for your specific application
  • File size typically between 50KB and 240KB (varies by country)
  • Correct pixel dimensions (varies by country and application type)
  • Taken within the last 6 months
  • Full face view directly facing the camera
  • Neutral facial expression or natural smile (depending on country requirements)
  • Both eyes open and clearly visible
  • Plain white or light-colored background
  • No glasses (unless worn daily for medical reasons)
  • No hats or head coverings (except for religious purposes)
  • Proper lighting with no shadows on face or background
