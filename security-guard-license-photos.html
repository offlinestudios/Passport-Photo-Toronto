Security Guard License Photo Requirements

The Ministry of the Solicitor General and the Canadian Police Information Centre (CPIC) have specific requirements for security guard license photos. Our service ensures your photos will comply with all these requirements:

  • Size: 50mm x 70mm (standard security license photo size)
  • Recent photograph taken within the last 6 months
  • Color photo with a white or light-colored background
  • Full face view directly facing the camera
  • Neutral facial expression
  • Eyes open and clearly visible
  • No shadows on the face or background
  • No glasses (unless medically required)
  • No head coverings (except for religious reasons)
  • Proper lighting with no red-eye effect

Security Guard License Application Information

While we specialize in security guard license photos, here's some general information about Ontario security guard licensing:

  • You must complete a 40-hour training course from an approved provider
  • You must pass the ministry test with a score of at least 62%
  • You must be legally entitled to work in Canada
  • You must be 18 years of age or older
  • You must have a clean criminal record or have received a pardon
  • The application process includes a background check
  • Licenses are valid for 2 years and must be renewed before expiry
  • Two identical photos are required for the application

For the most current information about Ontario security guard licensing, please visit the official Ontario Ministry of the Solicitor General website.
