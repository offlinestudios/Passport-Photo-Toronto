Work Permit Photo Requirements

IRCC has specific requirements for work permit photos. Our service ensures your photos will comply with all these requirements:

  • Size: 35mm x 45mm (standard Canadian work permit photo size)
  • Recent photograph taken within the last 6 months
  • Color photo with a white or light-colored background
  • Full face view directly facing the camera
  • Neutral facial expression with mouth closed
  • Eyes open and clearly visible
  • No shadows on the face or background
  • No glasses (as of 2021 IRCC regulations)
  • No head coverings (except for religious reasons)
  • Proper lighting with no red-eye effect

Work Permit Application Information

While we specialize in work permit photos, here's some general information about Canadian work permit applications:

  • Most work permits require a job offer from a Canadian employer
  • Some work permits require a Labour Market Impact Assessment (LMIA)
  • Processing times vary by country of residence and type of work permit
  • Applications can be submitted online or at a visa application center
  • Two identical photos are typically required for paper applications
  • Digital photos may be required for online applications

For the most current information about Canadian work permit applications, please visit the official IRCC website.
