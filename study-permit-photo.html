Study Permit Photo Requirements

IRCC has specific requirements for study permit photos. Our service ensures your photos will comply with all these requirements:

  • Size: 35mm x 45mm (standard Canadian study permit photo size)
  • Recent photograph taken within the last 6 months
  • Color photo with a white or light-colored background
  • Full face view directly facing the camera
  • Neutral facial expression with mouth closed
  • Eyes open and clearly visible
  • No shadows on the face or background
  • No glasses (as of 2021 IRCC regulations)
  • No head coverings (except for religious reasons)
  • Proper lighting with no red-eye effect

Canadian Study Permit Information

While we specialize in study permit photos, here's some general information about Canadian study permit applications:

  • You must have an acceptance letter from a designated learning institution (DLI) in Canada
  • You must prove you have enough money to pay tuition fees and living expenses
  • You must be a law-abiding citizen with no criminal record
  • You must be in good health and willing to complete a medical exam if required
  • You must convince an immigration officer that you will leave Canada when your study permit expires
  • Processing times vary by country of residence, but typically take 4-12 weeks
  • Two identical photos are typically required for paper applications
  • Digital photos may be required for online applications

Study Permit Application Tips

Here are some tips for a successful study permit application:

  • Apply at least 3-4 months before your planned start date
  • Ensure all documents are complete and accurate
  • Provide clear proof of financial support
  • Include a detailed study plan explaining why you chose your program and institution
  • Demonstrate ties to your home country to show you plan to return after your studies
  • Be honest and transparent in all aspects of your application

For the most current information about Canadian study permit applications, please visit the official IRCC website.
