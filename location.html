Parking

Metered street parking is available on McCaul Street and nearby streets. There are also several public parking garages within walking distance of our studio, including the one at OCAD University.

Getting Here

Our studio is easily accessible by various transportation methods

By Car

Our studio is easily accessible by car from the Gardiner Expressway and Don Valley Parkway. Street parking and public parking garages are available nearby.

By Public Transit

We are located within a 5-minute walk from St. Patrick Station. Several TTC streetcar and bus routes also stop within a block of our studio.

By Bicycle

Bicycle racks are available in front of our building. The area is well-connected to Toronto's bike lane network.

By Foot

Our studio is located in a pedestrian-friendly area of downtown Toronto with wide sidewalks and crosswalks at all major intersections.
