Canadian Passport Photo Requirements

The Government of Canada has specific requirements for passport photos. Our service ensures your photos will comply with all these requirements:

  • Two identical, unaltered photos taken within the last six months
  • Clear, sharp and in focus with a neutral facial expression (eyes open and mouth closed)
  • Taken against a plain white or light-colored background with uniform lighting and no shadows
  • 50mm x 70mm in size (2 inches wide x 2 3/4 inches long)
  • Head size from chin to crown must be between 31mm and 36mm
  • Show a full front view of the face and top of the shoulders squared to the camera
  • No glasses, hats, or head coverings (unless worn for religious or medical reasons)
  • Printed on high-quality photo paper (not heavyweight)
  • Black and white or color photos are acceptable

Canadian Passport Application Information

While we specialize in passport photos, here's some general information about Canadian passport applications:

  • Adult passports (16 years and older) are valid for 10 years
  • Child passports (0-15 years) are valid for 5 years
  • You can apply in person at a Service Canada location or by mail
  • Processing time is typically 10-20 business days for standard applications
  • Expedited and urgent processing options are available for additional fees

For the most current information about passport applications, please visit the official Government of Canada passport website.
