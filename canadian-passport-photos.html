Canadian Passport Photo Requirements

The Government of Canada has specific requirements for passport photos. Our service ensures your photos will comply with all these requirements:

  • Size: 50mm x 70mm (2 inches x 2¾ inches) - standard Canadian passport photo size
  • Recent photograph taken within the last 6 months
  • Color or black and white photo with a plain white or light-coloured background
  • Full face view directly facing the camera
  • Neutral facial expression with mouth closed
  • Eyes open and clearly visible
  • No shadows on the face or background
  • Face and shoulders centred and squared to the camera, not tilted
  • Face height between 31mm and 36mm from chin to crown of head
  • Clear, sharp and in focus with uniform lighting
  • No glare or flash reflections
  • Natural skin tone clearly visible

Canadian Passport Application Information

While we specialize in passport photos, here's some general information about Canadian passport applications:

  • Canadian citizens can apply for a passport at any age
  • Adult passports (16+) are valid for 10 years
  • Child passports (under 16) are valid for 5 years
  • Processing times vary: 20 business days for regular service, 10 business days for express
  • Two identical photos are required for the application
  • The photographer must sign, date, and print their name on the back of one photo
  • Photos must be taken by a commercial photographer or studio
  • Guarantor signature may be required depending on your situation

Common Passport Photo Mistakes to Avoid

Many passport applications are delayed or rejected due to photo issues. Common mistakes include:

  • Incorrect photo size (must be exactly 50mm x 70mm)
  • Shadows on face or background
  • Improper facial expression (smiling, frowning, etc.)
  • Poor lighting or contrast
  • Photos taken too long ago (must be within 6 months)
  • Missing photographer information on the back of the photo
  • Using home-printed photos instead of professional prints
  • Incorrect face positioning or size within the frame

Our professional service helps you avoid these common pitfalls, ensuring your application proceeds smoothly.
