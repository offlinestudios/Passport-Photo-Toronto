Digital ID Photo Requirements

Digital ID photos have specific requirements that vary by application type. Our service ensures your photos will comply with common requirements:

  • Digital format: JPEG/JPG file
  • Appropriate file size (typically between 100KB and 5MB)
  • Proper dimensions (varies by application)
  • Correct aspect ratio (typically square or 3:4)
  • High resolution (minimum 300 DPI)
  • Plain white or light-colored background
  • Full face view directly facing the camera
  • Neutral facial expression or natural smile
  • Both eyes open and clearly visible
  • No glasses (unless medically required)
  • No head coverings (except for religious reasons)
  • Proper lighting with no shadows on face or background

Digital Photo Tips

Here are some tips for getting the best digital ID photos:

  • Wear solid-colored clothing (avoid patterns, logos, or white tops)
  • Avoid heavy makeup or jewelry that may distract from your face
  • Style your hair so it doesn't cover your face or eyes
  • Arrive well-rested to avoid tired-looking eyes
  • Know the specific requirements for your application before your session
