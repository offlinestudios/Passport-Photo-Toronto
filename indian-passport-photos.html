Indian Passport Photo Requirements

The Indian government has specific requirements for passport photos. Our service ensures your photos will comply with all these requirements:

  • Size: 51mm x 51mm (2 inches x 2 inches)
  • Recent photograph taken within the last 6 months
  • Color photo with a white background
  • Full face view directly facing the camera
  • Neutral facial expression with mouth closed
  • Eyes open and clearly visible
  • No shadows on the face or background
  • No glasses (unless medically required)
  • No head coverings (except for religious reasons)
  • No uniforms
  • Proper lighting with no red-eye effect

Indian Passport Application Information

While we specialize in passport photos, here's some general information about Indian passport applications from Canada:

  • Indian passport applications from Canada must be submitted through BLS International Service Canada Inc.
  • Applications can be submitted in person or by mail to BLS centers in Toronto, Vancouver, Ottawa, and other Canadian cities
  • Processing times vary, but typically take 4-6 weeks for standard applications
  • Standard adult passports are valid for 10 years
  • Four identical photos are typically required for the application

For the most current information about Indian passport applications, please visit the official BLS International website.
