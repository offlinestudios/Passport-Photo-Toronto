PR Card Photo Requirements

IRCC has specific requirements for PR card photos. Our service ensures your photos will comply with all these requirements:

  • Size: 50mm x 70mm (standard Canadian PR card size)
  • Recent photograph taken within the last 6 months
  • Color photo with a white or light-colored background
  • Full face view directly facing the camera
  • Neutral facial expression with mouth closed
  • Eyes open and clearly visible
  • No shadows on the face or background
  • No glasses (as of 2021 IRCC regulations)
  • No head coverings (except for religious reasons)
  • Proper lighting with no red-eye effect

PR Card Application and Renewal Information

While we specialize in PR card photos, here's some general information about PR card applications and renewals:

  • New PR cards are typically valid for 5 years
  • You should apply for a renewal at least 6 months before your current card expires
  • Processing times vary, but typically take 40-60 days for standard applications
  • Two identical photos are required for the application
  • Photos must be taken within 6 months of your application
  • The photographer must sign, date, and print their name on the back of one photo

Common PR Card Photo Mistakes to Avoid

Many PR card applications are delayed or rejected due to photo issues. Common mistakes include:

  • Incorrect photo size
  • Shadows on face or background
  • Wearing glasses (no longer permitted in PR card photos)
  • Improper facial expression (smiling, frowning, etc.)
  • Poor lighting or contrast
  • Photos taken too long ago (must be within 6 months)
  • Missing photographer information on the back of the photo

Our professional service helps you avoid these common pitfalls, ensuring your application proceeds smoothly.
