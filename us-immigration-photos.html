U.S. Visa Photo Requirements

The U.S. Department of State has specific requirements for visa photos. Our service ensures your photos will comply with all these requirements:

  • 2x2 inches (51x51 mm) in size
  • Taken within the last 6 months to reflect your current appearance
  • Full face view directly facing the camera
  • Neutral facial expression or natural smile
  • Both eyes open and clearly visible
  • Plain white or off-white background
  • No glasses (as of 2016 U.S. regulations)
  • No hats or head coverings (except for religious or medical reasons)
  • Normal clothing (no uniforms or similar attire)
  • Proper lighting with no shadows on face or background

U.S. Visa Application Information

While we specialize in visa photos, here's some general information about U.S. visa applications:

  • Most non-immigrant visa applications require the DS-160 form
  • Applications typically require a visa interview at a U.S. Consulate or Embassy
  • Processing times vary by visa type and current workload
  • Application fees vary by visa type
  • Digital photos must be uploaded during the online application process

For the most current information about U.S. visa applications, please visit the official U.S. Department of State visa website.
